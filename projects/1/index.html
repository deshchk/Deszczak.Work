Karzdy Pomorze ‚Äì leveling up psychological research for my master's thesis

Video-making and coding a website to ultimatelly graduate with style.

TL;DR

See the project at karzdy.pomorze.pl

Research topic: Meaning in life, dimensions of loneliness and identity development in emerging adulthood ‚Äì read the thesis in Polish.

Achieved with: HTML, CSS, Alpine.js, vanilla JavaScript, Git, Github Pages, Google Sheets, Google Apps Script, VS code, Final Cut Pro, YouTube and other social media as well as others' help, insights and their word of mouth.

Characteristics:

  • Design (e.g. light/dark mode) and content (e.g. feminatives, following sections) change responsively on user's input or preference to assure the best User Experience.
  • Participants see their progress, are able to come back later as their answers persist and see just one section at a time for better data reliability and possibilities to take a break when needed.
  • Personalized results' interpretations generating on submit and staying visible even on going back to start.
  • Submit button gets removed on submitting and shows up again only after a complete reset to avoid spam, multiple submits and unreliable data.
  • Video introduction made for research explanation, potential participant encouragement and research marketing tailored to the target group (emerging adults).
  • Utilizing Jakob's law1 in the main form functionality for ease of use.

Effects:

  • 363 participants in 20 days, 293 in first two weeks and 142 in first 24 hours.
  • Lots of positive reviews (also from academics).
  • Many people willing to share the research and help make the site/interpretations better.
  • (intermediately) graduated with a 5 (grade A equivalent) ‚Äì got two As from the thesis' reviewers.
